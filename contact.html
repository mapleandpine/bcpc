How to Join BCPC

Never paddled before and want to try it? We welcome anyone with a competitive spirit and interest in working hard on and off the water. We paddle hard and we work hard to give back to the community.

Drop Coach an email to learn more about our team and year-round paddling: {{ site.email }}.

Please bring a signed BCPC Waiver to your first practice or complete the waiver at practice.

New paddlers have three free practices with Bridge City Paddling Club before they will be asked to complete the BCPC Membership form.

Paddlers must also join DragonSports USA after their third practice with the club.

See our Resources page for more information on the three steps to join Bridge City.
